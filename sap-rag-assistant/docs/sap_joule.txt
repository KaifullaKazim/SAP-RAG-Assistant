Title: SAP Joule

Joule is SAP's generative AI copilot embedded across SAP applications,
including S/4HANA, SuccessFactors, and BTP tools. It uses natural
language to let users query business data, trigger transactions, and get
contextual guidance directly inside SAP applications rather than
navigating multiple screens. Joule is built on top of SAP's Generative AI
Hub and AI Core infrastructure, meaning custom extensions and RAG-style
grounding built on BTP can, in principle, feed into or complement the
same underlying AI orchestration layer that powers Joule.
